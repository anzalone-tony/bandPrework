How I heard Car Seat Headrest...

So.. I heard Car Seat Headrest on an airplane while watching the Netflix show 'Flaked' starring Will Arnett. There was a scene where he will is walking aimlessly through Venice. He flaked on a date with one of his peers from AA. I think the lyric that caught my attention on that plane ride was "I want to kick my head in the shins". That was it. It was mindblowing for some reason. I would rewind the show to the beginning of the song and listen to it on repeat until the plane landed and I could take my phone out of airplane mode. I quickly googled the lyric from before and saw the band name Car Seat Headrest. I thought it was a terrible name and felt this would end up anticlimactic. I downloaded the album Teens of style and listened to the song a few more times. It was still just as good as the first time it played. There is a lot more to the story but I'm going to cut it a bit short. I loved them and still do. Below are videos to the songs I really enjoy. Hope you enjoy them too!
